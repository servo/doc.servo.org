TcpStream split support.

A TcpStream can be split into a ReadHalf and a WriteHalf with the TcpStream::split method. ReadHalf implements AsyncRead while WriteHalf implements AsyncWrite.

Compared to the generic split of AsyncRead + AsyncWrite, this specialized split has no associated overhead and enforces all invariants at the type level.
