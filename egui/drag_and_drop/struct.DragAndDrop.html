Tracking of drag-and-drop payload.

This is a low-level API.

For a higher-level API, see:

  • crate::Ui::dnd_drag_source
  • crate::Ui::dnd_drop_zone

  • crate::Response::dnd_hover_payload
  • crate::Response::dnd_release_payload
