Release 0.6.7 (2021-01-14)

  • Added a os::windows::Library::open_already_loaded to obtain a handle to a library that must already be loaded. There is no portable equivalent for all UNIX targets. Users who do not care about portability across UNIX platforms may use os::unix::Library::open with libc::RTLD_NOLOAD;
