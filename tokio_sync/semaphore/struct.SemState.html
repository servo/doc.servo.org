struct SemState(usize);
Expand description

Semaphore state

The 2 low bits track the modes.

  • Closed
  • Full

When not full, the rest of the usize tracks the total number of messages in the channel. When full, the rest of the usize is a pointer to the tail of the ‚Äúwaiting senders‚Äù queue.
