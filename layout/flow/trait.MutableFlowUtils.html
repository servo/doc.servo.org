fn collect_static_block_offsets_from_children(self)

Gathers static block-offsets bubbled up by kids.

This essentially gives us offsets of all absolutely positioned direct descendants and all fixed descendants, in tree order.

This is called in a bottom-up traversal (specifically, the assign-block-size traversal). So, kids have their flow origin already set. In the case of absolute flow kids, they have their hypothetical box position already set.
