The molad Beherad is the first molad, i.e. the molad of the epoch year. It occurred on Oct 6, 3761 BC, 23h:204ḥal (Jerusalem Time, Julian Calendar)

Which is the second Hebrew day of the week (Hebrew Monday), 5h 204ḥal, 2-5-204. (“Beharad” בהרד is just a way of writing 2-5-204, ב-ה-רד using Hebrew numerals)

This is 31524ḥal after the start of the week (Saturday 6PM)
