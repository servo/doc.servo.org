Fields§

§status_text: Option<String>§webviews: HashMap<TopLevelBrowsingContextId, WebView>

List of top-level browsing contexts. Modified by EmbedderMsg::WebViewOpened and EmbedderMsg::WebViewClosed, and we exit if it ever becomes empty.

§creation_order: Vec<TopLevelBrowsingContextId>

The order in which the webviews were created.

§focused_webview_id: Option<TopLevelBrowsingContextId>

The webview that is currently focused. Modified by EmbedderMsg::WebViewFocused and EmbedderMsg::WebViewBlurred.

§webview_preload_data: HashMap<TopLevelBrowsingContextId, WebViewPreloadData>

Pre-creation state for WebViews. This is needed because in some situations the WebViewOpened event is sent after ChangePageTitle and HistoryChanged
