§current_url: Option<ServoUrl>§current_url_string: Option<String>§webviews: HashMap<TopLevelBrowsingContextId, WebView>

List of top-level browsing contexts. Modified by EmbedderMsg::WebViewOpened and EmbedderMsg::WebViewClosed, and we exit if it ever becomes empty.

§creation_order: Vec<TopLevelBrowsingContextId>

The order in which the webviews were created.
